Bizzcard QRCode

BizzCard is an app to help you share your business card. In these days, we have a lot information to share with everybody about our work and skills. The business card still a powerful tool to show for present skills and do some networking. With this tool, you can add business cards and share your own, for other people.

It works like a repository of business cards.

Avoid to stock all your cards in your wallet. Use BizzCard for that!

Enjoy it!

ConsulTI Planilha

Gerencie suas consultas médicas com facilidade e envie por email. As consultas serão armazenadas em uma lista, no seu smartphone, e serão enviadas por email sempre que precisar. Também é possível limpar toda a lista, caso esteja obsoleta, e em seguida acrescentar novas consultas.
